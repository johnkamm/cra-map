Michigan Cannabis License Locations

Interactive Map of Adult Use & Medical Cannabis Licenses

About This Map

This interactive map displays Michigan cannabis license locations across the state. Licenses are categorized by type (Growers, Processors, Retailers, Transporters) and market (Adult Use vs Medical). Use the "Filter Licenses" panel in the top-right corner to toggle between Active/Inactive licenses and select specific license types. Click markers for detailed information about each location.

How to Use This Map

  • Zoom: Scroll or use +/- buttons to zoom in and out
  • Pan: Click and drag to move around the map
  • Clusters: Numbers show grouped markers; click to zoom in and expand
  • Markers: Click individual markers to see license details
  • Filter: Use the "Filter Licenses" panel (top-right) to toggle Active/Inactive and license types
  • Legend: View the legend (top-right) to understand marker colors and icons
